--- layout: home title: Dr. Xingjun Ma subtitle: Lecturer (Assitant Professor), School of Information Technology, Deakin University ---

I am a Lecturer in School of Information Technology at Deakin University and an Honorary Fellow at The University of Melbourne. My main research area is trustworthy machine learning, with the aim to design robust, secure and trusted machine learning models for AI applications.

I received my Ph.D. degree from The University of Melbourne, advised by Prof. James Bailey and Dr. Sudanthi Wijewickrema. I also worked for ~2 years at The University of Melbourne as a postdoctoral research fellow. I obainted my Bacholor and Master degrees from Jilin University and Tsinghua University, respectively.
