I am an associate professor of computer science at Fudan University and a member of the Fudan Vision and Learning Lab. I am also an honorary fellow at The University of Melbourne. My main research area is trustworthy artificial intelligence, which aims to design robust, secure, explainable, privacy-preserving, and fair machine learning models for modern AI applications. I am also interested in applying machine learning to solve scientific problems and expand our understandings of life, mind and the universe.

I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Computing and Information Systems at The University of Melbourne. Prior to Fudan, I worked for ~2 years at The University of Melbourne as a postdoctoral research fellow and ~1.5 years at Deakin University as a lecturer. I obtained my Bacholor and Master degrees from Jilin University and Tsinghua University, respectively.
