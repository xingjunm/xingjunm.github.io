I am a Lecturer in the School of Information Technology at Deakin University and an Honorary Fellow at The University of Melbourne. My main research area is trustworthy machine learning, aiming to design robust, secure and explainable machine learning methods/models for diverse AI applications.

I received my Ph.D. degree from The University of Melbourne, advised by Prof. James Bailey and Dr. Sudanthi Wijewickrema. I also worked for 1.8 years at The University of Melbourne as a postdoctoral research fellow. I obtained my Bacholor and Master degrees from Jilin University and Tsinghua University, respectively.
